  • Project Spotlight: Dairy-free chocolate cheesecake

    This is a dairy-free chocolate cheesecake that I created the recipe for my TECH 306 unit. This unit is a product design unit, which along with TECH 307, will go towards my food major. The requirement of the unit was to redesign a food product (a packaged food or recipe) and redesign the product and during that process, improve the product. The original product for this redesign was a woolworths cheesecake, with the need of the redesign to make the product dairy-free. There are limited dairy free cheesecake options available on the shelf, which made my redesign warranted. In the end the recipe was not only dairy-free but also vegan, as it was made with cashews and silken tofu.

  • Project Spotlight: Marquetry Box

    This project is a marquetry box that I made in TECH 205 - Design in Timber. This was the final physical project that we made in TECH 205, other projects include a chopping board and a low stool. The requirement for this project was to make a box, which had a marquetry component. I thorouhgly enjoyed this project and was proud of my final finished product. This box was a great project and something that I may make with a year 9 or year 10 timber class.

  • Project Spotlight: Bluey Birthday Cakes

    Currently I am undertaking a unit called TECH307, which relates to the unit TECH306. This is my major project unit. For my major project I have decided to design 3 different birthday cakes based on the kids tv Bluey. The recipes that I am designing will include all the steps taken to complete the cake design, photos of important steps and a copy of any templates that I used for the shapes/patterns. When completed this project will be shown in an exhibition which is run by the uni. I am excited to see how my final project turns out.
