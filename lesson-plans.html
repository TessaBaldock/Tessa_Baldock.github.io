Lesson Plans

Lesson Plans are plans that are made for specific lessons. These plans map out how each part of the lesson should go, as well as identify learning intentions and success criteria for the lesson. These plans allow time to be allocated to specific parts of the lesson and are designed to keep teachers on track to ensure that they get all content and activities for the lesson covered.

Teachers will follow different lesson plan templates, however they all do the same thing. The templates that these lesson plans follow identify things such as outcomes and AITSL standards that this lesson is meeting. The lesson is broken up into 3 parts- the introduction, the development and the conclusion. The lesson plans below are plans that I have made for different classes from the various placements that I have been on. The additional resources mentioned in the lesson plans can be found under other resources & tools tab of this website.
