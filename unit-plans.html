Unit Plans

Unit plans, are learning plans that have been developed for a term/whole topic area. The length of unit plans will often depend on the unit that is being taught. A good unit plan, has content that will be covered, the activities that will accompany the content that is being taught, the outcomes that it is meeting, as well as differentiated activities and extension activites.

As you spend more time in a school, your collection of unit plans will grow, whether that be due to making unit plans or being shared unit plans from other teachers. Good lesson plans are like gold, they are very valuable.
